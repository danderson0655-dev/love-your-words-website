## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Name | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Background | #FFFFFF | Pure White | Page background |
| Soft tint | #F7F4FE | Lavender Mist | Alternate sections |
| Pink tint | #FEF4FA | Blush | Warm/about sections |
| Text | #1C1830 | Deep Cosmos | All body copy |
| Primary | #7B52C8 | Amethyst | Buttons, links, accents |
| Accent | #D87DB0 | Sunset Rose | Gradient endpoint, highlights |
| Sky | #7BA8D8 | Soft Sky | Third gradient tone |
| Footer | #0E0B1E | Deep Night | Footer background only |

**Signature gradient:** `135deg, #7B52C8 0%, #D87DB0 100%`
Used on: logo text, hero headline, CTA buttons, credential bar.

## Logo Notes

No logo file yet. Placeholder in use.

**Logo vision (for designer):** "Love Your Words" in clean Outfit 800 weight, with the text rendered as a gradient from Amethyst (#7B52C8) to Sunset Rose (#D87DB0). Background imagery: universe/cosmos + flight/aerial. Text should feel bright and alive against the imagery.

---

## What This Brand Must Never Feel Like

- Brown, beige, or earthy warm tones
- Corporate or conservative
- Grandmother-adjacent
- Dark or moody (the footer is the only dark element)
- Generic nonprofit aesthetic
