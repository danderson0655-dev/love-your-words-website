How We Work Together

Retention copy gets built together. The words work because they are your story. True and real.

What the partnership looks like

You stay in the room. You bring the stories, answer the questions only you can answer, read every draft, and say so when a line sounds unlike you. Your people can tell when the words come from someone who was there. Projects run on schedule when that partnership holds, and drafts that sit unread move deadlines with them.

Your customers are the heroes

Retention copy is the copy that pays for itself. It grows from how you already serve: real problems solved, thank-yous that feel personal. The customer becomes the hero of the story, and your organization becomes the guide. From there the journey maps itself. You loved this project, so here's the news about what comes next.

The copy gets better with use

Words are a first draft of a relationship. Once a piece goes out, your numbers tell us what landed: opens, replies, renewals, second gifts. You know your data better than anyone. Bring it, and we test a headline or tighten an ask until the copy earns its keep.

Your stories stay yours

Seventeen and a half years in bank legal work taught me fiduciary duty and the weight of compliance. People's private matters stay private. Names and personal details stay out of the work unless you approve them. Your stories remain your property, start to finish. And copy that touches regulated ground gets written with your rules in mind.
