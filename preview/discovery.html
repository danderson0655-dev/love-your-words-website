A Discovery Call is thirty minutes. No pitch, no pressure. Just a real conversation about where your organization is, what your content is doing, and where the gaps are.

What to Expect

Thirty minutes. Three things.

01

I listen first.

Tell me about your organization, your audience, and the content challenge that brought you here. I'll ask questions. You'll do most of the talking. That's intentional.

02

We look at the gaps together.

Where is communication breaking down? What are you sending that isn't landing? What stories do you have that no one is telling yet? We'll look at the real picture.

03

We decide on next steps.

If there's a clear fit, I'll tell you what I'd recommend and how we'd work together. If there isn't, I'll tell you that too. No hard sell. No follow-up pressure.

Ready to schedule?

Pick a time that works for you. The call is thirty minutes via Zoom or phone -- whichever you prefer.

Common questions.

What if I don't know exactly what I need yet?

That's exactly what the call is for. Many of my clients come in knowing something isn't working -- they just aren't sure which piece to fix first. We'll figure that out together.

What does it cost?

Services are billed at $40 per hour. Package pricing is available for the full Customer Journey Content Package. We'll talk through what fits your situation and your budget on the call -- there's no obligation.

How quickly can you start?

Typically within one to two weeks of our first conversation. I'll give you a realistic timeline based on what you need and what my current schedule looks like.

Do you work with small organizations?

Yes. Many of my clients are small nonprofits, solo practitioners, or lean teams wearing multiple hats. That's the audience I know best and the one I'm built to serve.

What do you need from me to get started?

Not much. A conversation, a sense of your audience, and whatever existing content you have. I'll take it from there and ask for more as I need it. You won't have to explain your work from scratch.
