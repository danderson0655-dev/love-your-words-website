Common Questions

How much does this cost?

The Discovery Call is free. Project pricing depends on scope, and we talk numbers on the call once the work is clear. A written proposal arrives before anything starts.

How long does a project take?

It depends on the scope, and on how quickly drafts get read on your side. A single email sequence moves in weeks. A full package takes longer. Your proposal will carry a real timeline.

What if we're not sure what we need?

That's why we have this call. We figure out what makes sense for your organization right now: the full package, or one piece to start.

Do you work with organizations outside the nonprofit sector?

Yes. Nonprofits, health practices, essential-service businesses, and other mission-driven organizations that want to keep their people.
