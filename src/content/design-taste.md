# Design Taste

Preferences captured from design iteration sessions. Read this before making design decisions.

## Colors

- Dislikes warm earthy tones: brown, beige, warm ivory -- too much of it
- Wants bold, colorful, light and airy -- not muted or safe
- Drawn to sunset palettes: soft purples, lavender, dusty pink, soft gray-blue
- Loves water and the sky -- cool, expansive, open feeling
- White or very light background preferred (she specifically called this out)
- "Cosmic" and "universe" as visual reference points -- expansive, not earthbound
- Sunset-over-water palette: think purple twilight, rose clouds, soft silver sky

## Typography

- Simple, clean, very readable fonts -- priority is legibility for the Legacy niche (older readers)
- No heavy serifs, no decorative fonts that sacrifice readability
- Clean geometric or humanist sans-serif preferred
- Font sizes should be generous throughout

## Personality / Vibe

- Cool, explorer, wandering type
- 73 years old but does NOT want grandmother aesthetic -- she's adventurous, loves it all
- Bold and edgy in a light, airy way -- not dark or moody
- "Carried away into the heavens" -- spiritual, expansive, cosmic
- Water = freedom, transcendence, being carried somewhere bigger

## Components

- Hero imagery: universe + flight -- flying through air and stars
- Logo: "Love Your Words" in bright, bold text over universe/flight imagery
- Light backgrounds, spacious layouts

## Anti-preferences

- No brown, beige, or warm earthy tones anywhere
- No dark or moody designs
- No grandmother aesthetic
- No heavy serif fonts that feel old or stuffy
- No overly corporate or conservative feel
